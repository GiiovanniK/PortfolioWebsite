Inside-Outdoor

Website- development & design

For one of my clients, Inside-Outdoor, we were asked to design and develop a completely new company website. The website needed to be editable by the client themselves, so they wouldn't have to ask us to make changes in the code. Using the back-end CMS (Content Management System) WordPress, they can modify texts, images, and add new events on their own.

Figure 1: Home

For this project, I primarily focused on programming the website using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP. I also collaborated with the designers to refine some designs, making them more logical and easier to program. Once the website was fully programmed, we integrated it with WordPress to ensure that parts of the website would be editable by the client.

Figure 2: Offers

In the back-end, the client can customize various sections of the website, including texts, images, links, and the number of activity cards displayed. Additionally, the client has full control over customizing the main menu and footer of the website.

Figure 3: Back-end CMS

Naturally, the website was fully designed and developed with responsive mobile functionality in mind. It scales seamlessly across all devices to provide the best possible user experience.
